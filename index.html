What I do

Web Design

The creative process of designing a website or application is a key aspect to why I have enjoyed programming. It is essential to not only have websites that have relative and accurate information, but that information should be presented in a way that is easy for the user to navigate through because of its simplicity and clean design. Design should be responsive and a major tool to the brand or promotion of that person(s) or business. Examples of my design is this website portfolio and the wedding website found in the My Work section below.

Software/App Development

The aim of software development is simple: create technological solutions that benefits lives. That beneficial solution can occur in various ways because every client — whether a person or business — is different. It is important throughout development to keep a client centric approach that is simple, transparent and unique to meet that client's requirements and expectations. Careful planning along with innovative and curious minds will lead to an efficient and practical project solution. Two dashboard software project examples that I was a part of can be found in the My Work section below.
