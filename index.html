Web Design

The creative process of designing a website or application is a key aspect to why I have enjoyed programming. It is essential to not only have websites that have relative and accurate information, but that information should be presented in a way that is easy for the user to navigate through because of its simpicty and clean design. Design should be responsive and a major tool to the brand or promotion of that person(s) or business. Software/App Development

The aim of software development is simple: create technological solutions that benefits lives. That beneficial solution can occur in various ways because every client — whether a person or business — is different. It is important throughout the development to keep a client centric approach that is simple, transparent and unique to meet that client's requirements and expectations.
